Hey there, I'm Romie.

I am a passionate, self-taught front-end developer residing in Lufkin, Texas after changing careers from education to web development. In 2019 I graduated from college and started my teaching career. While I was teaching, I was also taking an interest in coding and quickly realized I had a growing passion for it. So at the end of the school year, I decided to fully commit to learning this new career field.
I started my coding career 6 months ago in October 2021 with SheCodes Workshops. What I find so fascinating about the field is when it comes to web development, I love the idea of creating something akin to art, that people can interact with. To create a webpage, is to in turn create a whole experience for the user and I want to be a part of that. I want to collaborate with people who are skilled in the field, learning and growing from them as well as being able to contribute my own ideas on a project. And I want to see first hand our design going from just a few brainstormed ideas on paper, to a fully functional app open to the public.
